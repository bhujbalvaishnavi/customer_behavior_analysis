# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes **3,900 customer transactions** to uncover meaningful insights into customer spending behavior, product preferences, and subscription trends.  
The goal is to translate raw data into **actionable business insights** that can improve customer retention, increase revenue, and optimize marketing strategies.

---

## 📊 Dataset Summary
- **Total Records:** 3,900 transactions  
- **Key Features:**
  - Customer ID
  - Age & Demographics
  - Product Category
  - Purchase Amount
  - Payment Method
  - Subscription Status
  - Purchase Date & Time  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Python:** Data cleaning, preprocessing, and EDA  
- **MySQL :** Business queries and data analysis  
- **Power BI:** Interactive dashboard and visualization  
- **Libraries:** Pandas, NumPy 

---

## 🔍 Key Steps

### 1. Data Cleaning & Preparation (Python)
- Handled missing values and duplicates  
- Standardized column formats and data types  
- Created derived features for deeper analysis  

### 2. SQL Analysis (Business Queries)
- Identified top-performing product categories  
- Analyzed customer purchase frequency  
- Segmented customers based on spending behavior  
- Evaluated subscription vs non-subscription trends  

### 3. Dashboard Creation (Power BI)
- Built interactive dashboards for key metrics  
- Visualized revenue trends and customer segments  
- Enabled dynamic filtering for business insights  

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- High-value customers contribute a **disproportionate share of revenue**  
- Certain product categories consistently drive **higher sales volume**  
- Subscription users show **higher purchase frequency and retention**  
- Peak purchase times reveal **clear customer buying patterns**  
- Payment method preferences vary across **customer segments**  

---

## 📈 Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on **high-value customer segments**  
- Promote **subscription plans** to increase retention  
- Optimize inventory for **top-performing product categories**  
- Use targeted campaigns during **peak purchase times**  
- Personalize offers based on **customer behavior patterns**
